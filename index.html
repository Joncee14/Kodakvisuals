About Me.

I’m Justice, the creative mind behind Kodak Visuals. With 4 years of experience as a photographer and graphic designer, I specialize in turning ideas into captivating visuals. Whether it’s capturing moments through the lens or crafting compelling designs, I’m passionate about bringing stories to life with creativity and precision. My work is inspired by the beauty in everyday moments and the power of bold ideas. Let’s create something extraordinary together and make your vision a reality!

Over the years, I’ve had the privilege of working as the graphic designer for notable enterprises in Nigeria, including Bignest Cafe, De Current Lands and Properties, Joncee Webs, Joncee Exchange, and many more. My clients consistently express their satisfaction with my ability to bring their ideas to life, delivering designs that not only meet but exceed their expectations. Their trust and positive feedback fuel my drive to deliver excellence every time.

Business Branding

Product Design

As a professional graphic designer i specialize in creating compelling flyers that help businesses stand out and connect with their audience.

Event flyers

Billboard Adverts

A good event flyers should be visually appealing, concise, and informative. it should feature a bold headlin, clear event details and eye-catching visuals that align with the event's theme. Proper use of colors, typography and layout ensures that flyer captures attention and encourages participation.

Delivery Enterprises

Brand Identity

A great delivery flyer should highlight your services clearly and attractively. Include essential details like contact information, delivery areas and special offers. Use vibrant visuals, bold headlines, and an organized layout tp grab attention and communicate your message effectively.

Traveling Flyers

Product Design

An effective traveling flyers should inspire adventure with stunning visuals and compelling content. Highlight destinations, packages and special offers, while ensuring contact details are easy to find. Use a clean layout, vibrant colors and engaging headlines to spark interest and drive bookings.

Season Sales

For special seansons

Season flyers should capture the spirit of the time with festive designs and relevant themes. Use vibrant colors, seasonal imagery, events or special offers. Keep the message clear and engaging to connect with your audience and drive interest.
